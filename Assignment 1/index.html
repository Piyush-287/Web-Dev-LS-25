My IITB Journey Timeline

Day 1: Entering July 2024

It was a rainy day. The streets were all wet.A vagabond entered the arena of warriors.The hallways were all busy by but still the splash of water can be heared and peircing through your ears as you enter the campus . The Sky was still in anger of unknown .The traveller said goodbye to his parents waving his hands and pouring his heart in that one moment as he was first time going to be alone for maybe a week , a month, or forever ... As the night falls , the wanderer slept on his bed wondering all the various things going around him waiting for a new sunrise ...

The New BeginningFirst Sem

As time passed by the stranger made a lot of companions and freinds along the arena. They used to play and do parties at night.Go on expenditure outs and do various things together.

The return of FEAR EndSem 1

The vagabond was unaware of the consequences of his actions that were going to come later . As the season end arrived all the warriors kept back on their training and achieving different fighting skills . But the lost stranger kept on enjoying his days with his new companions and never cared enough to fear the death that was waiting for him ahead. When the finals were approaching the wanderer along with his companions used to go on practice even at night and challenges stars to join them as they dare to make fun of them . They used to climb up the mountains and see how far and brave the moon is .

A voyage far to the eastPost end sem 1

The wanderer along with his friends cleared the vast cloud of smoke that was ahead of him somehow. In the excitement of this they decided to go to a venture far to the east and enjoy . The venture was too fun to even end but the last ray of sun also disappeared. The traveller got back to his hometown and returned after a break of a month

Royal Appointment Feb-April

The second season of travellers journey was far different from first he encountered the fall and rise of a new monarch . All the ministries were hence therefore electing new members . The fight for new employment was immense and hence the traveller realised his limitations.
